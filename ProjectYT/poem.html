Whispers of the Dawn

Lies a world painted in hues of gold.
The whispering wind, a gentle tune,
Dances with shadows beneath the moon.
The stars, like secrets, softly gleam,
Guiding the hearts that dare to dream.
The ocean hums its endless song,
A rhythm where weary souls belong.
Through forests deep and valleys wide,
Hope blooms where shadows seek to hide.
Each dewdrop kissed by morning's grace,
Holds the story of an endless chase.
For life’s a journey, wild and free,
A fleeting echo, a timeless plea.
To love, to learn, to rise, to fall—
To cherish the beauty within it all.
